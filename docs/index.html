WHY PASSNOOK

Simple enough for anyone.
Secure enough for everyone.

Everything you need to keep your passwords safe — without the cloud, without a subscription, and without having to be a tech expert.

AES-256 Encryption

Your vault is encrypted with your master password using PBKDF2-SHA256 and Fernet. Every password you save is unreadable without your key.

Stays on Your Computer

Your vault is a single encrypted file on your PC — it never touches a server. No cloud sync, no accounts, no telemetry. You own it completely.

Passwords & Secure Notes

Store website logins with usernames and passwords, or save anything as a secure note — Wi-Fi passwords, PINs, recovery codes, anything sensitive.

One-Click Copy

Copy your username or password to the clipboard with one click. The clipboard clears automatically after 30 seconds so nothing lingers.

Password Generator

Generate strong, random passwords with configurable length and character sets — uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. One click to use it.

Auto-Locks

Locks after 30 minutes of idle time and hides to the system tray when you close it. Your vault is never left open and unattended.

HOW IT WORKS

01
